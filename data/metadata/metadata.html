Section 1 Overview
Originator Minnesota Department of Transportation
Title City Boundaries in Minnesota
Abstract Federal Urban Code lists the U.S. Census federal adjusted urban area a road segment is assigned to.

The adjusted urban boundaries from the 2010 U.S. census.

Check other metadata records in this package for more information on Boundaries of Federal Adjusted Urban Area.
Purpose To identify whether an area is large urban (>50,000), small urban (5,000 to 50,000) or rural (<5,000).

Federal Urban Code was developed to fill a need at Minnesota Department of Transportation for a continuous, statewide GIS base map of Federal Urban Codes throughout the State.
